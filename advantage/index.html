Why Ivolv
We are often asked where the name Ivolv, came from.
Well, it’s time we explain:

The theory of evolution developed by Charles Darwin and others, states that all species arise and develop through the selection of small, inherited variations that increase the individual's ability to compete, survive, and reproduce.
We chanced upon reading this theory, again, and found several parallels with the constantly changing business world. With the upsurge of digital technologies, businesses have to evolve - compete, survive and grow.
When we were brainstorming names with friends, someone quietly wrote IVOLV on the whiteboard ( implying EVOLVE) and the word stuck. There's no fancy story or clever spin on the name, it just popped up on the whiteboard and we loved it…so did many others.
