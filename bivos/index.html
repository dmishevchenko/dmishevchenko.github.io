BIVOS
Banking Analytics Suite
BIVOS is our flagship Analytics Suite for Banking & Financial Services. It is designed to give you and your customers secure and quick access to the information they need to make informed business decisions. Disparate data sources, both structured and unstructured, are linked and crunched to empower business users with self-service analytics. The ultimate goal being, to uncover patterns and trends that leads to actionable insights.
BIVOS offers to:

  • Brings together your business data globally, across the various sectors, products and systems, in a single unified view.
  • Manages risk and mitigates loss.
  • Identifies and exploits new opportunities and new markets by uncovering patterns and trends in your data.
  • Predicts future behaviour, and models the likely outcomes.
  • Provides business users with managed self-service analytics in real-time, for increased customer engagement on preferred device.
