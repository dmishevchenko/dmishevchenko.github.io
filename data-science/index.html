DataOps Methodology
At Ivolv, we employ DataOps, to rapidly deliver unified data platform that supports agility, enables and accelerates integration of operations and analytics. Focussing on an agile approach, DataOps supports collaboration and automation between the data scientists, engineers, operations and IT teams. DataOps promotes continuous model deployment, repeatability, productivity gains and self service as it significantly improves the overall security, goverance and quality of data analytics.
